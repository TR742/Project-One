<strong>Dog friendly Things to do in Arizona </strong>

Arizona also known as the Grand Canyon state is the 14th most populated state in the United States. The state is most associated with its hot arid climate the state is in fact home to a diverse array of biomes from the Canyons, or the northwest to the snowy mountain ranges, and pine forests near Flagstaff. Millions of tourists visit Arizona every year and there are many opportunities to include your furry friends on these adventures.

<strong>Hiking and Camping </strong>

Arizona has many opportunities for outdoor recreation. Whether you want to explore the Grand Canyon, the beautiful landscape of Sedona, or the Saguaro cactus forest, why not take your friend with you on the adventure. Pack plenty of water, and be mindful of the wildlife, and plan your trips out.
