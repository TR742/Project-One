Dog Friendly Dinners to Take Your Furry Companions!

O.H.S.O Brewery

Address: 4900 E Indian School Rd, Phoenix, AZ 85018

O.H.S.O has numerous locations across the valley that is fully accepting of accomidating dogs in their restaurants! This is a great place to bring your pets and dogs are even accomidated with their own water bowls!

Luci's at the Orchard

Address: 7100 N 12th St building 2, Phoenix, AZ 85020

Luci's is a great restaurant that is completely dog friendly. With a current rating of 4.5 stars on Google, this is a wonderful location to have a bite to eat with your companion.

Farm and Craft Scottsdale

Address: 4302 N Scottsdale Rd, Scottsdale, AZ 85251

Farm and Craft is a beautiful inside and outside location with great food and service. Dogs are welcome on the premise and is a great spot to stop to get a quick meal or a few drinks while enjoying the weather.

Ocotillo

Address: 3243 N 3rd St, Phoenix, AZ 85012

Ocitillo is notorious for their outside patio's style and service, allowing for a dog friendly environment. This patio restaurant makes it easy for customers to bring their furry friends and have a compatible and wonderful dinning experience!

Snooze

Address: 15054 N Scottsdale Rd #110 D1B, Scottsdale, AZ 85254

Snooze is a great breakfast location, priding themselves on inclusivity. This ideology of the restaurant doesn't just include different types of people, rather species in general. Their accessible patio makes this a great way to start your morning with your pets!
