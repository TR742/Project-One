Places to Go to Take Your Favorite Companions!

Hance Park Dog Park

Address: 323 W Culver St, Phoenix, AZ 85003

This dog park is a great place to take your pet to burn energy and socialize! Busiest around 5pm, this is either a great time to wait if you want to be alone with your pup, or an opportunity to make new friends!

Vista del camino off-leash area

Address: 7800 E Pierce St, Scottsdale, AZ 85257

If you are comfortable with letting your best friend roam free, this might be a great option for you! This dog park is tolerant to allow dogs to roam free off their leash and explore! Great place to bring social dogs!

Camelback Hike

Address: n 85018, 5700 N Echo Canyon Dr, Phoenix, AZ 85018

This hiking location is great to bring your pets to if they enjoy long walks outside. Avoid bringing dogs in the height of the summer without protective paw gear!

Papago Park

Address: 625 N Galvin Pkwy, Phoenix, AZ 85008

Another great park for your dogs that includes a little bit of everything. Consists of resting locations for owners with fields for their buddies to play. Also includes hiking trails for friendships who enjoy long walks together!

Always Unleashed Pet Resort

Address: 7230 E Adobe Dr, Scottsdale, AZ 85255

This pet resort is a business and does require a membership, however, is a great place to drop off your friends if time away as an owner is needed. This resort offers a swimming pool for dogs to splash and play!
